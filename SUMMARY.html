2,000 Step LLM Speedrun Summary

Architecture & Parameters

The final model architecture replaces the mediocre baseline with a highly optimized GPT configuration, staying strictly under the 2,000,000 parameter limit.

  • Vocabulary: Custom Byte-Pair Encoding (BPE) Tokenizer trained on train_corpus.txt with vocab_size = 4096 and ByteLevel fallback. This compresses sequence lengths (especially Hindi/Devanagari) significantly over raw bytes.
  • Embeddings: Token dimensions n_embd = 144, block size block_size = 256. Token embeddings and softmax output weights are tied (tie_weights = True), saving ~600k parameters.
  • Layers: n_layer = 5 transformer blocks.
  • Attention Heads: n_head = 6 heads per block.
  • Total Parameters: 1,880,496
  • Initialization: Standard GPT-2 residual scaling initialization applied (std = 0.02 with residual projections scaled by 1/sqrt(2 * n_layer)).

Training Setup & Optimizer

The training loop was heavily refactored to maximize data exposure and stability within 2,000 steps.

  • Optimizer: AdamW with weight decay of 0.1, replacing standard Adam.
  • Learning Rate Schedule: Cosine Annealing with a 10% linear warmup (200 steps). Peak LR: 1e-3.
  • Gradient Clipping: Norm clipped to 1.0 to prevent destabilization early in training.
  • Effective Batch Size: Increased from 8 to 32 by processing batches of 16 with 2 gradient accumulation steps, yielding 8,192 tokens per step. Over 2,000 steps, the model trains on ~16.3 million tokens.
