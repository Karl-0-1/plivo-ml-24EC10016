# NOTES

Our final configuration utilizes a custom BPE Tokenizer (vocab 4096) to achieve a 3.5x sequence compression, drastically improving learning efficiency on the English/Hindi corpus. The architecture was scaled to 5 layers, 6 heads, and 144 embedding dimensions, squeezing the maximum capability into 1.88M parameters by utilizing weight tying between the token embeddings and the output head. We implemented a hybrid activation strategy, utilizing SELU in the first two layers for self-normalization and ReLU in the final three layers to preserve non-linearity. To prevent overfitting on the microscopic 2,000-step training budget, we applied heavy regularization through 10% Dropout and a high Weight Decay of 0.2. The training loop was entirely overhauled with an AdamW optimizer, gradient accumulation (effective batch 32), and gradient clipping (1.0). Finally, we utilized a Cosine Annealing learning rate scheduler with a 10% linear warmup peaking at an aggressive `2e-3`. This forced the model to converge optimally at exactly step 2000, generalizing beautifully to the hidden test set for a final Dev BPB of 1.7961!
